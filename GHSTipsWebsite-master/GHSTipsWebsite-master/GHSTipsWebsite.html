Grant High School

Grant

Grant High School is a public highschool in Portland Oregon named after President Ulysses S. Grant. The school oppened in 1924 and is located at 2245 NE 36th Avenue, Portland Oregon, 97212.

This website is designed to give new students at Grant a short and clean introduction to the school's classes, food options, and culture. After interviewing teachers and students, this webstie features the information every new Grant freshman should know.

However, as of 2018, Grant High School is currently opperating at the Marshall campus. This website has been built with the originial Grant campus in mind so it remains usefull into the 2020s.
