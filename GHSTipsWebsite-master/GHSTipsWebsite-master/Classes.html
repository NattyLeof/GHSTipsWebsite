Themes in Contemporary Literature: Let's Play!

"Themes in Contemporary Literature: Let's Play! is a senior English and composition course that explores contemporary literature—essays, novels, memoirs, plays, journalism, poems, documentaries, and feature films—through the lens of play. In addition, this year long course consists of comprehensive instruction in the craft of argumentative writing and offers students the opportunity to earn Writing 121 credit through Portland Community College." -Mr. Hawking

AVID

"This college readiness program is nationally known for helping students become academically prepared for college, complete all the college related efforts (college research, applications, financial aid, and more), and supporting each students individual needs." -Ms. Wolfer

PSU Prob & Statistics

" Interested in getting a better idea of how probability & statistics are used in the field of math, science, psychology, business, etc.? Earn 8 college credits in this year long course that includes cooperative group work, exploration of theoretical statistics and daily games/challenges to make your senior year math class fun, interesting and applicable!" -Ms. Wolfer
